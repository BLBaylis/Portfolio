About Me

Hi, I'm Brad and I'm from Essex in the UK. I've always been interested in many different topics, especially maths, philosophy and science.

What I didn't consciously realise until my late teenage years was that this stemmed from a general enjoyment of learning new things and this passion led me to achieve a bachelor's degree in Mathematics with the Open University, which is a distance learning university.

The same desire to learn has led me down the path of self-taught web-developer! Some other interests of mine include gaming, running, climbing and cycling.
